Signing Up for ImPACT - Step 2


Dear Researcher,

Congratulations on completing the first step of the enrollment:

  • Please check your email (including Spam/Junk) for a confirmation email from ImPACT COmanage Registry. Follow the instructions in the email to confirm your email address by clicking the indicated link.
  • The link will take you back to the COmanage site (you will once again login using CI Logon). Once your email is confirmed you will be redirected back to this site for more instructions.

Please note: you will always use your institutional credentials to login to the ImPACT Notary Service via your institution's Identity Provider - we do not and will never store your password or login information. Any password changes that you go through with your institutional account will also affect how you login to the ImPACT Notary Service.
