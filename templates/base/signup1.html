Signing Up for ImPACT - Step 1


Dear Researcher,

Welcome to ImPACT! Before you sign up we would like you to familiarize yourself with our project Infrastructure for Privacy-Assured CompuTations.

In order to begin using the ImPACT Notary Service you will have to take the following steps:

  • Click ‘Proceed’ and you will be redirected to a CI Logon page, where you can select your institution and login using your institutional username and password.
  • After logging in you will be redirected to an enrollment page in ImPACT’s COmanage Registry which will display your name, email and institution. Please verify those and you will be redirected back to this site for more instructions.

Please note: you will always use your institutional credentials to login to the ImPACT Notary Service via your institution's Identity Provider - we do not and will never store your password or login information. Any password changes that you go through with your institutional account will also affect how you login to the ImPACT Notary Service.
